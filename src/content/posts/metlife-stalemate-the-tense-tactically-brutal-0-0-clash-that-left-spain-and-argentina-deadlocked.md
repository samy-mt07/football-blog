Argentina, on the other hand, looked to exploit the transition. Every time Spain’s counter-press faltered, Argentina attempted to launch rapid vertical thrusts targeting the spaces behind Spain's high defensive line. Yet, Aymeric Laporte and Robin Le Normand put on an absolute clinic in recovery defending, snuffing out the danger before Argentina’s forwards could get a clean look at goal. It was a masterclass in tactical discipline from both sides, resembling a high-stakes chess match where neither grandmaster was willing to make the first fatal error.
