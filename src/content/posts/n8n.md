Deadlock in Vancouver
Switzerland and Colombia played out a scoreless draw through normal and extra time in their FIFA World Cup Round of 16 clash at BC Place in Vancouver. Neither side found the net across the full ninety minutes plus the additional periods, with the match ending 0-0 on the scoreboard.
Decided on Penalties
With the sides unable to be separated, the tie went to a penalty shootout. Switzerland converted their chances at the crucial moments, winning the shootout 4-3 to advance in the tournament. The match, played on July 7, 2026, was officially recorded as a full-time result, sending Switzerland through to the next round of the FIFA World Cup.
