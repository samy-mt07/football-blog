A Tightly Fought Contest
The Round of 16 meeting between Switzerland and Colombia at the FIFA World Cup 2026 lived up to its billing as a tense affair. Staged at BC Place in Vancouver, Canada on July 7, 2026, the match saw both teams cancel each other out over the full ninety minutes and extra time.
Spot-Kicks Have the Final Say
With the score still 0-0 after extra time, the outcome came down to a penalty shootout. Switzerland held their nerve to win it 4-3, securing their place in the next stage of the tournament. The result was confirmed as full-time once the shootout concluded.
